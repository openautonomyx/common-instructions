# Pricing Plan

## Editions

### Community
- Price: `$0`
- Includes: open-source core code, self-hosting, BYO API key.
- Excludes: closed-source `common-instructions` bundles, managed improvement loops, premium policy packs, air-gapped support.

### Pro
- Price: `$49/month` per workspace.
- Includes: closed-source `common-instructions` access, monthly improvement-loop updates, standard guardrail/testing/process policy packs, email support.
- Excludes: air-gapped/offline bundle.

### Team
- Price: `$199/month` per workspace (up to 10 users).
- Includes: everything in Pro, weekly updates, priority triage, advanced review/eval packs, SLA-backed support.
- Excludes: air-gapped/offline bundle.

### Enterprise
- Price: custom (`starting at $999/month`).
- Includes: everything in Team, custom policy packs, private registries, air-gapped/offline bundles, SSO and compliance add-ons, dedicated onboarding and roadmap.

## Access and Entitlements
- Open-source code remains public.
- `common-instructions` and improvement loops are private and subscription-gated.
- Air-gapped operation support is premium and Enterprise-gated.
- Feature and update entitlements are tier-based.
- Entitlements are version-channel scoped (for example: `stable`, `weekly`, `custom`).

## Billing and Renewal
- Plans auto-renew monthly unless canceled before renewal date.
- Access is provisioned per workspace.
- Upgrade takes effect immediately with prorated billing.
- Downgrade applies at next billing cycle unless otherwise agreed.

## Expiry and Revocation
- If a paid plan expires, private package/update access is revoked.
- Previously downloaded artifacts remain usable under license terms in effect at download time.
- New updates, premium registries, and managed loops require an active paid plan.

## Change Control
- Pricing changes require notice before renewal.
- New premium capabilities may be added to higher tiers without reducing active-tier entitlements.
